## ⚙️ Basic Configuration

To start trading, you need to set up the bot with your preferences. Here is what you typically need to do:

- **Connect your wallet:** The bot requires access to your Solana wallet to trade tokens. Use your wallet’s seed phrase or private key only if you trust the bot software and its environment.  
- **Choose tokens:** Select which Solana tokens or memecoins you want the bot to watch and trade.  
- **Set trading rules:** Define simple rules like how much to buy or sell and when (e.g., price limits, timing).  
- **Start the trading process:** Press the "Start" button to let the bot begin working automatically.
